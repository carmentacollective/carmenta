# Carmenta Vision

Carmenta is the best interface to AI. The unified front door to artificial intelligence
for people who build at the speed of thought.

## Why Carmenta Exists

In a world where AI can build anything, the bottleneck shifts from engineering capacity
to clarity of thought. The interface to AI becomes the leverage point for everything
else.

Current tools are fragmented. ChatGPT, Claude.ai, Cursor, Claude Code. Each good, none
complete. Context doesn't persist. Conversations start fresh. There's no team, just you
and a chat window.

Carmenta changes this. Memory that persists. Context that compounds. A team of AI
specialists working alongside you.

## The Name

Carmenta was a Roman goddess credited with inventing the Latin alphabet. She adapted
Greek letters into the writing system that would carry Western civilization's knowledge
for millennia. The alphabet may be the most transformative technology in human history:
it enabled law, literature, science, and the transmission of ideas across time and
space.

She was also the goddess of prophecy and protector of mothers in childbirth. The one who
sees what's coming and guides the vulnerable through transformation.

Carmenta is technology in service of human flourishing. That's the product we're
building.

## Core Capabilities

<memory>
The best context and memory management available. Carmenta remembers what matters. Who you are. What you're working on. What you've decided. Who you know. What you've learned.

The AI always has the context it needs. No more explaining your situation every
conversation. </memory>

<voice>
Voice as a first-class citizen, not an afterthought. Talk to Carmenta, talk with Carmenta. Voice that feels natural and actually works.
</voice>

<model-selection>
Default: Let Carmenta choose. A preprocessing layer analyzes the request, picks the right model, the right temperature, the right response strategy. The user doesn't think about models.

Optional control: Speed/quality slider (swift, balanced, deep) or manual override for
specific model selection. </model-selection>

<ai-team>
The Digital Chief of Staff is the first team member. She tracks commitments across conversations, maintains the knowledge base, anticipates needs before they're expressed, handles operational coordination while you focus on what only you can do.

Additional team members follow: Researcher, Analyst, Creator, Reviewer. Each
specialized. All working from shared context. </ai-team>

<scheduled-agents>
Agents that run on your schedule, not just when you ask. Daily briefings. Hourly monitoring. Weekly research digests. Carmenta works while you sleep, surfaces what matters when you wake.

Combined with proactive intelligence: preparing for upcoming meetings, watching for
signals that need attention, escalating what matters, handling what doesn't.
</scheduled-agents>

<service-connectivity>
Native integrations for services that matter: Notion, ClickUp, Gmail, Slack, Google Drive, GitHub, and more. One subscription covers full connectivity. MCP servers remain supported for custom integrations.
</service-connectivity>
